Best Places to Visit in Winter ❄️

Gulmarg ⛷️

⭐ 4.9 Rating

About: Gulmarg is one of India's most famous winter destinations known for its heavy snowfall and skiing activities. It looks like a winter wonderland during snowfall.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹18,000

Top Activities: Skiing, snowboarding, cable car ride

Shimla 🌨️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Shimla is a popular winter destination known for snowfall, colonial architecture, and scenic mountain views.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹14,000

Top Activities: Snowfall watching, toy train ride, mall road visit

Tawang 🏔️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Tawang is a hidden winter gem in Arunachal Pradesh known for its snowfall, monasteries, and peaceful environment.

Best Time: November to February

Budget: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000

Top Activities: Monastery visits, snowfall photography

Sonmarg ❄️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Sonmarg is famous for its glaciers, snowy mountains, and breathtaking winter scenery.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹15,000

Top Activities: Glacier visits, snow rides

Narkanda 🌲❄️

⭐ 4.6 Rating

About: Narkanda is a peaceful hill station known for snowfall and apple orchards.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Snow trekking, skiing

Pahalgam ❄️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Pahalgam is a beautiful winter destination in Kashmir known for snowy landscapes, pine forests, and peaceful surroundings.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹16,000

Top Activities: Snow walks, horse riding, sightseeing

Khajjiar 🌨️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Khajjiar is known as the Mini Switzerland of India. It becomes extremely beautiful during winter with snowfall and green meadows covered in snow.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹13,000

Top Activities: Snow photography, nature walks

Lachung 🏔️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Lachung in Sikkim is a hidden winter paradise known for heavy snowfall, scenic valleys, and peaceful surroundings.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹17,000

Top Activities: Snow sightseeing, valley exploration

Kufri ⛷️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Kufri is a famous winter destination near Shimla known for snowfall and winter sports activities.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Skiing, snow rides

Sela Pass ❄️🏔️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Sela Pass is a stunning high-altitude mountain pass in Arunachal Pradesh known for heavy snowfall, frozen lakes, and breathtaking Himalayan views.

Best Time: December to February

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹16,000

Top Activities: Snow photography, sightseeing, mountain views
