Best Places to Visit in Monsoon 🌧️

Munnar 🌿

⭐ 4.9 Rating

About: Munnar is famous for its tea gardens, misty hills, and cool weather. During monsoon, the greenery becomes even more beautiful.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹15,000

Top Activities: Tea garden visit, waterfall sightseeing

Cherrapunji 🌧️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Cherrapunji is one of the wettest places on Earth, known for waterfalls, caves, and lush greenery.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹14,000

Top Activities: Waterfall visits, cave exploration

Lonavala 🌄

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Lonavala becomes extremely scenic during monsoon with waterfalls, green hills, and cloudy weather.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Trekking, waterfall visits

Coorg 🌲

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Coorg is known for coffee plantations and waterfalls that look magical during monsoon.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹13,000

Top Activities: Coffee plantation tours

Valley of Flowers 🌸

⭐ 4.9 Rating

About: Valley of Flowers becomes colorful and vibrant during monsoon.

Best Time: July to September

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹16,000

Top Activities: Trekking and photography

Mahabaleshwar 🍓

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Mahabaleshwar is known for misty hills, waterfalls, and strawberry farms.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Sightseeing and nature walks

Wayanad 🌿

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Wayanad is known for forests, waterfalls, and wildlife.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹14,000

Top Activities: Wildlife visits and trekking

Udaipur 🌧️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Udaipur becomes romantic and scenic during monsoon with lakes filled.

Best Time: July to September

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹15,000

Top Activities: Boat rides and sightseeing

Mount Abu 🌄

⭐ 4.6 Rating

About: Mount Abu is Rajasthan’s only hill station and looks beautiful in monsoon.

Best Time: July to September

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Boating and sightseeing

Agumbe 🌧️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Agumbe is known as the rainforest of South India and is famous for heavy rainfall.

Best Time: June to September

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹13,000

Top Activities: Rainforest trekking
