Best Places to Visit in Summer ☀️

Manali 🏔️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Manali is a beautiful hill station known for its cool climate and adventure activities.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000

Ladakh 🏔️

⭐ 4.9 Rating

About: Ladakh is one of the most breathtaking summer destinations in India known for its stunning mountains, Pangong Lake, and adventurous road trips.

Best Time: May to September

Budget: ₹15,000 - ₹25,000

Top Activities: Bike riding, Pangong Lake visit, camping

Tirthan Valley 🌲

⭐ 4.6 Rating

About: A peaceful and less crowded valley perfect for nature lovers.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000

Lansdowne 🌄

⭐ 4.5 Rating

About: A quiet hill station with beautiful scenic views.

Best Time: March to June

Budget: ₹5,000 - ₹9,000

Spiti Valley 🏞️

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: A cold desert valley known for adventure and stunning landscapes.

Best Time: May to September

Budget: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000

Ooty 🌼

⭐ 4.6 Rating

About: A beautiful hill station known for greenery and cool weather.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Chopta 🏞️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Chopta is a peaceful and less crowded hill station known as the "Mini Switzerland of India." It offers stunning mountain views and fresh air.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹6,000 - ₹11,000

Top Activities: Trekking, camping, nature photography

Kodaikanal 🌲

⭐ 4.6 Rating

About: Kodaikanal is a charming hill station known for its lakes, waterfalls, and cool climate, making it a perfect summer getaway.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000

Top Activities: Boating, cycling, sightseeing

Munnar 🌄

⭐ 4.8 Rating

About: Munnar is famous for its lush green tea plantations, cool weather, and peaceful atmosphere. It is one of the most beautiful summer destinations in South India.

Best Time: March to June

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹13,000

Top Activities: Tea garden visits, sightseeing, photography

Auli 🏔️

⭐ 4.7 Rating

About: Auli is a beautiful hill station known for its scenic mountain views, cable car rides, and peaceful surroundings. It remains cool during summer and is perfect for nature lovers.

Best Time: April to June

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹14,000

Top Activities: Ropeway ride, trekking, sightseeing
